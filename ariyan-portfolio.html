Company Profile

Factory: Mawna, Tengra, Sreepur, Gazipur, Bangladesh.
Office: House # 4 (2nd Floor), Road # 12, Nikunja # 02, Dhaka # 1229, Bangladesh.

Established in 2017, Ariyan Socks Industries Ltd. is a 100% export-oriented private limited company specializing in socks and tights manufacturing. We serve clients across Asia, Europe, USA, and Canada.

Products & Market

  • Dress Socks, Sports Socks, Kids Jacquard & Tights (any design/pattern)
  • Anti-Bacterial, Diabetic, Anti-slip, Knee High, Ankle, Sneaker, Fake, and 3D Socks
  • Custom rubber part knitting (1X1, 2X1, 3X1, Pique), various heel & toe types

Major Buyers: 4F (Poland), New Yorker (Germany), Spring Field (Spain, HK), Atlas for Men (France), Camp David (Germany), Red Tape (India), SOS (Denmark), Mammut (Denmark), Carry (Poland), Marubeni Fashion Link (Japan), Stanley (UK), Power Tek (Canada), Legea (Italy), and more.
