Ariyan Socks Industries Ltd. is a 100% export-oriented private limited company, established in 2017 and located in Mawna, Tengra, Sreepur, Gazipur, Bangladesh. Commercial production started in 2017.

We produce all kinds of dress socks, sports socks, kids jacquard & tights with any design and pattern, exporting to Asia, Europe, USA, and Canada.
