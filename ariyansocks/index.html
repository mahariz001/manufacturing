Welcome to Ariyan Socks

Leading hosiery & socks manufacturer in Bangladesh. Ariyan Socks Industries Ltd is based in Mawna, Tengra, Sreepur, Gazipur. We are a fast‑growing, 100% export‑oriented private limited company focused on delivering high‑quality hosiery products and unmatched customer service.

Since our foundation in 2015, our team of dedicated professionals has combined innovative design with cutting‑edge technology to produce premium socks for markets around the world. As a proud member of the Bangladesh Garment Manufacturers and Exporters Association (BGMEA), we continually strive to exceed industry standards and remain a trusted global supplier.

About Us

We produce socks using advanced knitting equipment, rigorous quality control and sustainable sourcing. Our corporate office is located in Dhaka and our state‑of‑the‑art factory operates from Gazipur. We leverage world‑class machinery and skilled staff to ensure every pair meets the highest standards. We invite you to explore our facilities, learn about our product range and discover why clients around the world choose Ariyan Socks.
