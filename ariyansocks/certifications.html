Our Certifications

As a responsible manufacturer, Ariyan Socks Industries Ltd complies with international standards and undergoes regular audits to demonstrate our commitment to ethical business practices, product safety and environmental stewardship. Below are summaries of our principal certifications.

amfori BSCI (Business Social Compliance Initiative)

The amfori BSCI audit evaluates labour standards, working conditions and human rights within our facility. The most recent partial follow‑up audit confirmed that our factory, established in 2015, meets requirements throughout all stages of production — knitting, linking, finishing, pressing and packing — and maintains consistent volumes year‑round.

SEDEX (SMETA) Audit

Our SMETA (Sedex Members Ethical Trade Audit) covers labour standards, health and safety, environment and business ethics. The audit includes all categories of workers — employees, agency staff and contractors — ensuring that our operations align with the highest ethical standards.

Formaldehyde Free Certification

To verify the absence of hazardous substances, we conduct formaldehyde testing on finished goods. One recent test carried out from 10 May 2020 to 24 May 2020 measured free formaldehyde in cotton and wool fabrics according to the Japanese Health & Welfare Ministry Ordinance No. 34 (1974), certifying that our socks are free from harmful levels.

OEKO‑TEX Standard 100

Ariyan Socks Industries Ltd holds an up‑to‑date OEKO‑TEX certification, which guarantees that our products are free from substances harmful to human health and comply with strict European safety standards.
