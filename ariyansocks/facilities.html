Our Facilities

Knitting Machines

We operate over 150 single‑cylinder knitting machines with 6‑feeder drums sourced from Dong Sung of Korea and Da Kong of Taiwan. These machines, featuring electronic yarn sensors and step‑motor adjustments, allow on‑the‑fly design changes and precise control over cylinder, stitch and heel settings. A full expansion will increase capacity to 300 machines across two floors.

Linking Machines

Our linking department is equipped with nine Dong Sung machines from Korea. These machines ensure secure and comfortable linking of toe seams for finished socks.

Steam & Finishing Machines

We utilize HELIOT‑SOKMAGIC boarding machines for steam treatment and shaping. Our automatic boarding systems, drum finishers and textile finishing tumblers from Heliot Turkey deliver perfect finishing, softening and antibacterial treatments for every pair.

Metal Detector Machines

Each sock passes through a sensitive UK‑made metal detector to identify any broken metal parts after knitting and linking. Our lock‑system detectors can spot fragments as small as 1 mm, ensuring product safety and compliance.

Raw Materials

We source premium yarns from Bangladesh, Taiwan, Korea, Indonesia, India and China. Our materials include 100 % cotton, cotton/modal blends, viscose, CVC, PC, lurex, polyester, nylon, bamboo and various covered elastane and rubber yarns. Collaborating with trusted suppliers allows us to meet diverse quality requirements.

Quality Assurance

Our research and development team sets production standards to guarantee that every finished product meets or exceeds customer specifications. Rather than relying solely on end‑line audits, we encourage in‑station quality control, giving each operator ownership of the quality of their output.

Ready‑Made Garment Sector Compliance (RSC)

The RSC Inspection Team has fully surveyed Ariyan Socks Industries Ltd. We adhere to all relevant safety and compliance requirements, ensuring a safe and ethical working environment.
